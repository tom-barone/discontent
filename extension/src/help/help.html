But what do you do about all those nasty rotten spammers?

There are some checks in place to stop abuse:

  • You can only vote on a maximum of 10 websites a day
  • You can only ever have 1 vote on a website
  • If there are a bunch of suspicious votes from suspicious users, the system can flag it, ban the users and remove the votes.
